    About our beloved candidate Mr. Star

    Our candidate is a beloved star fish with a heart of gold and a passion for serving others.

    He is known for his determination, creativity, and unshakeable optimism, and has a proven track record of making a positive impact in the lives of those around him. Despite his goofy demeanor, Patrick has a heart of gold and often goes out of his way to help his friends. He may not be the brightest star in the sky, but he is a loyal and dependable companion to SpongeBob and the other residents of Bikini Bottom.
